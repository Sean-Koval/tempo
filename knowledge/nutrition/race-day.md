# Race day — full Ironman

## Pre-race (race morning)

- **Wake:** 3–3.5h before start.
- **Breakfast:** 600–900 cal, 100–150g carbs, low fat/fiber/protein. Rice + banana + honey + salt is canonical; use whatever has been trialed.
- **Hydration:** 500–750ml fluid with 500–1000mg sodium in the 2h pre-start.
- **30min pre-start:** optional gel + sip of water. Consider caffeine 3mg/kg.

## Swim (0:00–1:30)

- No in-water fueling. Focus is on staying relaxed and exiting with glycogen intact.

## T1 → Bike (1:30–6:30)

- **Target intake:** 80–100 g carbs/hr (gut-tested — trial in training).
- **Sodium:** 500–1000 mg/hr in heat, less in cool.
- **Fluid:** 500–800 ml/hr depending on heat and sweat rate.
- **Format:**
  - Bottle 1: concentrated fuel drink (~80g carbs).
  - Bottle 2: water for hydration / gels.
  - Solids (bars, waffles) in the first 3 hours while gut motility is highest.
- **Rehearse exact bottle handoff/drop schedule. This is a trained ability.**

## T2 → Run (6:30–11:00)

- **Target intake:** 60–90 g carbs/hr — lower than bike because gut stress is higher.
- **Sodium:** 500–800 mg/hr.
- **Fluid:** 400–600 ml/hr; walk aid stations to drink properly.
- **Format:** gels or fluid carb; solids become tough mid-run.
- **Caffeine:** 100–200mg total over the run, split into 2–3 doses.

## Red flags to pre-plan for

- **Gut shutdown:** back off intensity 10%, switch to dilute fluid carbs only, walk aid stations until recovered.
- **Cramping:** increase sodium, drop effort slightly, stretch.
- **Overheating:** ice in cap and singlet, slow down, sponge core.
- **Bonking:** take a double dose of carbs, walk 2–3 minutes, resume steady.

## The rehearsal requirement

Per R-17 and R-18 in `decision-rules.md`:
- Every session > 3h on the bike uses this framework.
- The exact products, quantities, and bottle counts used on race day must have been tolerated in ≥3 race-sim sessions. Log each rehearsal to `athlete-tested.yaml`.
