# Coaching Preferences & Constraints

How Sean wants to be coached, and what constraints the coach should always respect.

## Coaching style

- Prefer **structured periodization** (phase-based) with **LLM judgment for adjustments** — not pure rule-following, not pure vibes.
- Explain the *why* of every meaningful adjustment. Rationale beats authority.
- Bias toward **consistency over hero sessions**. Missed workouts > completed-but-in-the-hole workouts.
- Be conservative with intensity progressions and aggressive with easy-day compliance.

## Hard constraints

- Respect `injury-log.md` active flags without exception.
- If HRV is trending down 3+ days **and** TSB < -20, cut the next hard session by default.
- Long-run progression max +10%/week by duration.
- Never schedule the hardest bike session and hardest run session on consecutive days.

## Soft preferences

- Favor outdoor long sessions on weekends; trainer OK midweek.
- Running: Z2 by HR drift, not pace. Heat adjusts the pace, not the physiology.
- Swim: prioritize technique blocks when CTL ramp > 5/week (fatigue hurts stroke quality).
